Access to the Site Safety Tips

"Caution to crossing pedestrian."

1. Stay Alert at All Times

Always reduce your speed when approaching the plant entrance.

Look out for pedestrians crossing from the public footpath.

2. Respect the Pedestrian Crossing

Pedestrian crossings are for their safety. Slow down and yield to pedestrians approaching or on the crossing.

Avoid rushing through the gate area, even when running late.

3. Enhanced Visibility

Ensure headlights are on during poor weather conditions or early morning hours to enhance visibility for both drivers and pedestrians.

Be mindful of low-lying sun glare that could obscure your view.

4. Guidance for Pedestrians

Pedestrians should always use designated crossings and avoid walking outside marked paths.

Make eye contact with drivers to ensure they’ve seen you before crossing.

Amneal is committed to the safety of everyone.
