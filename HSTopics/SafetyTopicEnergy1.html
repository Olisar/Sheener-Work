Amneal Energy Insights

Powering Progress at Amneal

Smart energy choices drive sustainability, cost savings, and reliability for everyone here.

Compressed Air – Hidden Costs and Big Wins

Compressed air is vital, yet energy hungry. Routine leak tracking and swapping air blowers for vacuum pumps have cut waste this year. Over 865,000 kWh saved in 2025, with 400,000+ kg less CO₂. Every correctly set cleaning run and leak report counts.

Monthly walkdowns and smarter controls help us keep costs down and reliability up.

Smarter HVAC – Bigger Savings

Our cleanrooms and process areas are powered by HVAC. New SOPs mean units are off on non-production days: no wasted power, always within spec. Ongoing projects in 2025: better zoned controls, filter management, weekend/holiday setbacks. Results: large electricity and gas savings, unlocking ongoing improvement.

Everyone sticking to schedules keeps us compliant and sustainable.

Every Action Counts

Every staff member makes a big impact by reporting leaks, sticking to shutdown plans, switching off idle kit. That’s how Amneal keeps saving energy. Recent feedback and audits show innovation and vigilance across the team lead to big gains.

Small steps turn into major results for Amneal, the environment, and the future.
