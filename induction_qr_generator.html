📱 How to Use:

  2. Enter IP above: Look for "IPv4 Address" (e.g., 192.168.1.100)
  3. Generate QR: Click the button to create your QR code
  4. Scan with phone: Use your phone's camera to scan the QR code
  5. Complete quiz: Answer the safety questions on your phone

Note: Your phone must be on the same WiFi network as your computer.
