Safe Work, Shared Responsibility!

Every role matters together, we create a safer workplace for all.

Employer Duties: Leading a Safe Workplace

Employers must take all reasonably practicable steps to ensure the safety, health, and welfare of everyone at work. This includes providing a safe environment, conducting risk assessments, maintaining equipment, supplying training and supervision, and reporting incidents as required. Employers are also responsible for preparing a safety statement and ensuring that safety measures do not cost employees financially.

Employee Responsibilities: Playing Your Part in Safety

Employees are required to look after their own safety and that of others by following workplace procedures, using equipment correctly, and cooperating with safety measures. They must never work under the influence of intoxicants, report hazards or unsafe conditions promptly, and participate in training and safety activities as directed by their employer.

Directors & Senior Management: Setting the Safety Standard

Directors and senior managers are expected to demonstrate visible leadership on health and safety, ensuring that robust policies, resources, and monitoring systems are in place. They are legally accountable for health and safety performance and can be held personally liable if failures are due to their neglect or consent.

Safety Representatives: Your Voice in Health & Safety

Safety Representatives act as a bridge between employees and management on health and safety matters. They participate in inspections, investigations, and meetings, raise concerns on behalf of staff, and help drive improvements. Their role is protected by law, and they are entitled to appropriate training.

Shared Commitment: Building a Culture of Safety Together

Workplace safety is a shared responsibility involving everyone—employers, employees, contractors, and suppliers. Collaboration, consultation, and mutual respect are essential for identifying hazards, managing risks, and ensuring a safe and healthy environment for all.
