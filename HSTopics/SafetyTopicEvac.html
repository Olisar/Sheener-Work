Evacuate Smart. Stay Safe Together.

When every second counts, your actions matter: take your card, follow exit signs, and stay alert.

Step 1: Grab Your Employee Card Immediately

When you hear the fire alarm, promptly take your employee swipe card before leaving your workstation. You’ll need this card at the assembly point to confirm your safe evacuation via the swipe system, which records who is safely outside the premises.

Step 2: Follow Emergency Exit Signs Out

Carefully follow the nearest illuminated emergency exit signs to the closest designated exit door. Do not delay, move calmly and quickly, without returning for belongings, ensuring your path remains safe and unobstructed.

Step 3: Go to The Nearest Assembly Point and Swipe Your Card

Proceed directly to the nearest assembly point outside the building. There, use your swipe card at the provided reader so the system marks you as present and safe at the assembly area.

Step 4: Listen to ERT Members’ Instructions

Remain at the assembly point and listen to all instructions from the Emergency Response Team (ERT) members present. They are there to ensure everyone's safety and coordinate any further actions during the evacuation.

Step 5: Stay Until Official Permission to Leave

Do not leave the assembly point until given permission by the designated ERT member at the Muster Point. Only once the ERT confirms everyone’s safety and authorises departures may you leave or return to the building.
