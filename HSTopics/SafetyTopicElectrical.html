Festive Fire Safety Tips

Stay Fire-Safe This Festive Season

Protect Your Home, Family, and Community

1. Test Your Smoke Alarm

Ensure smoke alarms are working. Test them weekly and replace batteries when needed.

2. Decorate Safely

Keep decorations away from open flames and heaters. Use fire-safe lights with CE marks.

3. Avoid Overloaded Sockets

Plug in lights and devices responsibly. Overloading sockets increases fire risk.

4. Stay Alert with Candles

Never leave candles unattended. Place them away from flammable items like curtains or trees.

5. Check Open Fires

Use a fireguard and clean chimneys before lighting open fires or stoves.

6. Extinguish Cigarettes

Fully extinguish cigarettes and ash. Avoid smoking in bed or near flammable materials.

7. Plan Your Escape

Have a clear fire escape plan. Get out quickly and call 999 or 112 if a fire starts.
