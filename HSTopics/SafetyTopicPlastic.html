Plastic Waste Reduction

Powering a Plastic-Free Future

Small changes in our daily habits lead to a significant reduction in our environmental footprint.

The Global Plastic Challenge

Over 300 million tons of plastic are produced annually, much of which pollutes our oceans and fills landfills. At Amneal, we are committed to sustainability by minimizing single-use plastics and promoting circular economy principles. Reducing waste protects our local ecosystems and preserves resources for future generations.

Rethink, Refuse, Reuse

Simple steps at work make a big difference. Use refillable water bottles and coffee mugs instead of disposable cups. Choose metal cutlery and reusable containers for your lunch. Before ordering supplies, consider if there is a plastic-free alternative or if packaging can be reduced. Together, we can eliminate thousands of single-use items every year.

Responsible Recycling

Not all plastics are created equal. Ensure you are using the correct recycling bins for clean plastics and keeping them free from food contamination. Proper sorting ensures that high-quality material can be reborn into new products. Your vigilance at the bin is the final step in closing the loop on plastic waste.
