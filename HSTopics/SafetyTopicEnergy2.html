Amneal Energy Optimisation Achievement

Optimisation Upgrade Success

Through strategic management and team dedication, we've secured significant energy savings and financial returns.

The Achievement

Through the fantastic management of the Amneal Optimisation Upgrade, our Energy team has secured a significant return from Energia. We implemented targeted carbon footprint reduction projects across our facilities, appointed an SEAI-accredited audit company, and completed a comprehensive facility-wide energy audit. This milestone represents our commitment to sustainability and operational excellence.

The Results

Energy Saved: 1,992,255 kWh

Financial Credit: €129,500 from Energia

These results are equivalent to powering over 400 Irish homes annually. Every kilowatt saved demonstrates how strategic energy management delivers both environmental and financial benefits for Amneal's future.

How We Achieved This

1. Identified energy optimisation opportunities across all facilities
2. Appointed an SEAI-accredited audit company
3. Implemented comprehensive carbon reduction projects
4. Submitted detailed audit report to Energia
5. Secured €129,500 financial credit

This systematic approach ensures compliance, maximises savings, and builds a sustainable energy framework for continued success.
